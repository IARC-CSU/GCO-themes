Errata

January 2015

The incidence rates of breast and prostate cancer estimated in France in 2012 have been modified following the publication of data by the National Cancer Institute and the French Institute of Public Health Surveillance (InVS).

These changes slightly affect the eight regional estimates which include France in terms of incidence and prevalence. The modified results are presented in this version (1.2) of the GLOBOCAN 2012 database.

November 2014

An error has been found in the data used to estimate cancer mortality in Estonia, resulting in unrealistically low estimates.

The corrected figures are presented in the version (1.1) of the GLOBOCAN 2012 database. Should you find any errors or inconsistencies please contact us at: csu@iarc.fr.
